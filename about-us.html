We are here for you

Virtual grader is a platform were academic institutions can register and avail the services. This services include online gradering, contestation, transparency, storing institutions/student academic records. Faculty members would be able to grade/marking students assessment online. On the other hand student would be able to view there assessment scripts and can report an issue at any time. Higher authorities of academic institutions have rights to virtually look over the marking report of an assessments.
There will be a Android scanning app for capturing the assessment scripts. And the web portal for students, teachers and academic members.
